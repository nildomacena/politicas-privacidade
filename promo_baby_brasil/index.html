Privacy Policy - Promo Baby Brasil

English

The Promo Baby Brasil application is a platform for promoting deals on children's products. The app does not collect or share any mandatory user data.

Our app only displays promotions and offers for infant and children's products available in the Brazilian market.

Users may optionally enable notifications for specific categories of products they are interested in. These preferences are stored locally on the user's device and are not shared with third parties.

No personal information is required to use the basic functions of the application.

For any questions or additional information, please contact us by email: koinedevelopment@gmail.com.
